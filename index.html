🌴 Coronado Island — half day
10–15 min drive over the iconic Coronado Bridge, or take the Coronado Ferry from Broadway Pier (more fun!). Full afternoon of beach + exploring.
What to do on Coronado
🏖️ Coronado Beach One of the most beautiful beaches in the US — wide, soft sand, glittering water from mica minerals. Swim, lay out, take photos with Hotel del Coronado as the backdrop.
🏰 Hotel del Coronado stroll Iconic Victorian resort, completely worth walking through even if you're not staying. Shops inside, great for photos, afternoon drinks on the beach patio.
🛍️ Orange Ave shopping Main street of Coronado — boutiques, gift shops, gelato. Very walkable, charming small-town feel. Great for bachelorette-themed trinkets.
🌿 Spreckels Park 601 Orange Ave · beautiful shaded park, perfect for a quick picnic break or to relax before heading back. Gazebo, local neighborhood charm.
⛴️ Ferry back at golden hour Take the Coronado Ferry back to Broadway Pier for stunning views of the downtown skyline at sunset. Best views of the trip.
